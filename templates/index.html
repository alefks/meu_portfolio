how and why I started?

I was 15 years old (2018) when I started to learn how to code, and my father realized that I was learning by myself, only watching youtube videos. He wanted me to get to know better the world of I.T, so he took me to a beginner course here in my hometown (Praia Grande, city in the state of São Paulo in Brazil). That's where everything started. From that day on, I have never got tired of learning more and more about this amazing world. The possibility of helping others through technology really delights me.
